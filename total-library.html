The Infinite Library

When I was a kid, I asked my dad this question: since drawing is just putting atoms on a canvas, could you generate every single combination of color on a canvas to create every single picture in the world possible?

I think I originally asked that question because I heard about scientists devising formulas to explain the universe and if you did this, you would probably eventually find that formula right?

Well that idea was instantly shot down by the fact that there's going to be too much useless information to filter out to find anything meaningful

Anyways here I am, a decade later, making that except with text.

This is similar to The Library of Babel but I'm pretty sure my single digit age self didn't mean to plagarize some idea he never heard about.

This is the nth book in the whole library
